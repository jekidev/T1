# Denmark world knowledge

Denmark is the default world profile for Urban Strategy Simulator.

## Administrative structure

- Country: Denmark
- Regions: Capital Region, Region Zealand, Region of Southern Denmark, Central Denmark Region, North Denmark Region
- Municipalities are the main local administrative units used for city services and local planning.
- Default language: Danish (`da-DK`)
- Default currency: Danish krone (`DKK`)
- Default timezone: `Europe/Copenhagen`

## Default cities

- Copenhagen: national capital and largest metropolitan area
- Aarhus: major city in Central Denmark
- Odense: major city on Funen
- Aalborg: major city in North Denmark
- Esbjerg: major city on the west coast of Jutland

## Simulation dimensions

Scenario designers may model:

- population and density
- road, rail, bicycle and public-transport networks
- ports, airports and logistics hubs
- municipalities, districts and neighborhoods
- weather, daylight, season and time of day
- economy, services and infrastructure
- events, missions, resources and territorial zones

This knowledge is descriptive game context. Time-sensitive statistics, laws, schedules and operational data should be retrieved from approved current sources rather than assumed from this file.
